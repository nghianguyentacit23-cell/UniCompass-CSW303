# Product Vision: UniCompass - AI Academic Copilot

UniCompass is a proactive academic copilot that helps university students make clearer and more confident study decisions. Instead of waiting until registration deadlines, low grades, or delayed graduation risks appear, students can use UniCompass to understand their current academic situation, plan the next semester, check whether a course choice is suitable, explore possible consequences of failing a course, and evaluate whether a target GPA is still realistic.

In the current course project scope, UniCompass focuses on three practical student needs: semester planning, course eligibility awareness, and GPA goal checking. The product helps students see recommendations and warnings in simple language so they can act earlier and avoid common mistakes such as choosing the wrong course, underestimating workload, or discovering academic risks too late.

In the long-term vision, UniCompass can become an Academic Operating System for students. It can support the full university journey from first-year orientation to graduation planning, career-oriented course selection, internship readiness, academic risk monitoring, and personalized study guidance. The goal is not only to provide information, but to become a trusted academic companion that helps students make better decisions throughout their university life.
